About

Bioinformatician and molecular biologist with a multidisciplinary profile integrating molecular biology, human genetics and advanced computational transcriptomics. My research spans four lines: genetic determinants of metabolic disease, molecular genomics of sinonasal cancers, gene regulatory mechanisms in peripheral nerve disorders, and systems‑level regulation in liver disease. Expert in NGS data analysis, alternative splicing, pipeline development and multi‑omics integration.
